How Ayurveda works
In Ayurveda diagnosis of illnesses done by means of checking different bodily functions. An Ayurvedic Doctor checks your Prakriti (temperament) pulse, stool, urine, tongue, speech, touch, vision, and appearance.
Ayurveda is life science used for achieving a healthy balance between body, spirit, and mind.

Ayurveda believes, when there is balance of three doshas, your healing energies are at their strongest.

Ayurvedic treatment is a means of promoting and maintaining good health, along with correction of disease.

Ayurveda proclaims that everything in the universe is connected, and so when body, spirit and mind are connected and harmonized, optimum health and vitality will follow.
